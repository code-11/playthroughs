The Old Gods - Setup

I recently obtained the Crusader Kings 2 expansion: Old Gods. This expansion makes pagans playable and pushes the start date of the game earlier.

Crusader Kings has a steep learning curve, but is very engaging once you understand the system. For those interested in learning, it takes perhaps one playthough of a game in order to learn the mechanics. However, because of how the game works this may take several real life days.
